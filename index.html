When did burritos first appear in the United States?

Burritos were first mentioned in the US in the early 20th century, with the first recorded US mention appearing in a 1934 Mexican cookbook, but the real boom came in the 1960s and 70s when fast food chains and the iconic Mission-style burrito helped popularize them nationwide.
